What We Do

HalalBarcelona is an independent travel guide built to help Muslim visitors and residents enjoy Barcelona with confidence. We bring together everything you need in one place — verified halal restaurants, Muslim-friendly hotels, mosques and prayer spaces, and practical tips for traveling in Spain while keeping to your values.

Barcelona has a thriving Muslim community of well over 100,000 people and a fast-growing halal food and travel scene. But that information is often scattered across dozens of sites and reviews. Our goal is to gather it, check it, and present it clearly so you can spend less time searching and more time enjoying your trip.

Why You Can Trust Us

We research every restaurant, hotel and mosque we list using community reviews, local knowledge and publicly available information. Halal certification in Spain varies — some venues are formally certified, others are Muslim-owned without paperwork — and we always encourage readers to confirm directly when it matters to them.

We're based in the Barcelona area ourselves, which means many of our recommendations come from real, on-the-ground experience rather than guesswork.

How We Fund This Site

HalalBarcelona is free to use. To keep it running, some links on this site are affiliate links — for example, hotel booking links. If you book through one of them, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects which places we recommend; our listings are based on quality and usefulness, not commissions.
