Travel Tips
Muslim Travel Tips for Spain
🕌

Friday Prayer

The main mosque in Barcelona is Centre Cultural Islàmic de Catalunya. Several smaller mosques operate in El Raval neighborhood.

🌙

Ramadan in Barcelona

Many halal restaurants stay open late during Ramadan. El Raval neighborhood has the highest concentration of halal eateries.

✈️

Getting Around

Barcelona has an excellent metro system. Most halal restaurants are accessible via L1 (red line) and L3 (green line).

🛒

Halal Grocery Shops

El Raval and Sant Pere neighborhoods have many halal butchers and grocery stores for self-catering travelers.
